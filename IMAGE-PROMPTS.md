# Robot Shares — Image Prompts for Google AI Studio

15 prompts ready to generate. Once you have the images, save them to `img/` and replace the `<div class="image-placeholder">` blocks in the HTML with `<img>` tags.

Recommended settings: 16:9 aspect ratio, high quality, photorealistic or concept art style as noted.
